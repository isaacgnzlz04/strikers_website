## Animations

1. **Scroll In** (GSAP)
   - Cards fade in from opacity 0
   - Slide up from 60px below
   - Scale from 0.9 to 1
   - Stagger: 0.1s between cards

2. **Card Hover**
   - Transform: translateY(-5px)
   - Box-shadow appears
   - Transition: 0.3s ease

3. **Modal Open**
   - Backdrop fades in
   - Modal scales from 0.9 to 1
   - Content fades in

4. **Toggle Switch**
   - Background color transition
   - Sliding animation
   - Duration: 0.3s
